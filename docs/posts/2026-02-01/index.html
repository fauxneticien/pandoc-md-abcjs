Vashon backing

In the Vashon Celtic Tunes chart for Out on the Ocean, the Part B starts out with the Em chord:

Out On The Ocean
Jig in G

Part A
G G | G D | G G | C D
G G | G D | G G | D G

Part B
Em Em | Em Em | D D | D D
G  G  | C  G  | G G | D G
Em Em | Em Em | D D | D D
G  G  | C  G  | G G | D G

Perhaps obvious?

Perhaps the choice of vi: E minor: (E - G - B) is a no brainer, given the melody lands right on the E note at both the 1 and 4 down beats (jig in 6/8).
